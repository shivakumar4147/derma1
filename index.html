Dermatology Services in Mangaluru

Every concern is different. Each service below is designed to solve a specific problem with realistic, step-by-step care.

General Skin Consultation in Mangaluru

Many patients visit Skin 360 after trying home remedies or over-the-counter creams that did not work. During a consultation, Dr. Prashanth listens to your history, examines your skin in detail, and explains what is actually happening beneath the surface.

You leave with a clear diagnosis, a written plan, and simple instructions you can follow at home.

  • Personalised plans for acne, pigmentation, allergies, and infections
  • Guidance on what to stop using and what to continue
  • Follow-up reviews to track improvement over weeks, not days

Chronic Skin Disease Management

Conditions like eczema, psoriasis, urticaria, and vitiligo can come and go for years. At Skin 360, the goal is not just temporary relief — it is long-term control with fewer flares.

Treatment plans balance medical prescriptions, lifestyle advice, and maintenance routines that fit into your daily life.

  • Stepwise protocols to reduce itching, scaling, and redness
  • Education on triggers and how to avoid them in Mangaluru’s climate
  • Realistic expectations: what can fully clear, and what needs ongoing care

Hair Loss & Scalp Treatments

Hair fall can feel alarming and emotional. Instead of quick fixes, Skin 360 focuses on understanding why you are losing hair — from nutrition and hormones to lifestyle and genetics.

Treatments combine medical therapy, advanced procedures, and scalp health routines.

  • Structured evaluation of pattern hair loss, diffuse thinning, and dandruff
  • Clinic-based PRP and growth factor sessions when indicated
  • Documented progress with photos so you can see the change

Laser Hair Removal in Mangaluru

Laser hair reduction at Skin 360 uses FDA-approved technology, with parameters adjusted for Indian skin tones. The focus is on safety first, then comfort, then long-term reduction.

Each session is planned so you know what to expect — how many sittings, gap between visits, and visible milestones.

  • Patch test before starting full treatment
  • Personalised settings for face, underarms, arms, legs and bikini area
  • Detailed aftercare to minimise bumps, tanning, or irritation

Skin Polishing & Glow Treatments

For patients who say “my skin just looks dull,” polishing and glow sessions gently clear dead cells, smoothen texture, and help skincare products work better.

Instead of promising overnight fairness, the clinic focuses on healthy, even-toned, confident skin.

  • Microdermabrasion and medically curated peels
  • Protocols for brides, events, and important life moments
  • Maintenance plans to keep the glow after the procedure

Advanced Aesthetic Dermatology

Ageing is natural — but you can choose how gracefully it appears on your skin. Advanced care at Skin 360 includes anti-ageing peels, scar revisions, and regenerative procedures.

Every aesthetic recommendation is made only after understanding your comfort level and long-term goals.

  • Protocols for fine lines, open pores, and acne scars
  • Step-by-step approach instead of aggressive one-time procedures
  • Honest counselling about what is possible and what is not
